How to Play

HDRdle is a daily hitbox guessing game for HewDraw Remix.

Rules:

  • You'll see a hitbox visualization from a move
  • Guess the character AND the move (in any order)
  • You have 12 total guesses
  • Green = correct, Red = incorrect
  • Win by correctly guessing both!

Hints:

  • Spend guesses to reveal helpful information
  • Show Skeleton (-1 guess) - reveals the character's pose
  • Show Frame # (-1 guess) - shows which frame the hitbox is on
  • Show Hurtboxes (-2 guesses) - reveals the character's hurtboxes

A new puzzle is available every day at midnight!
