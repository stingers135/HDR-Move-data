Welcome to HDR Move Data

Explore detailed attack data and frame information for various characters in HewDraw Remix. Damage and knockback values shown are the strongest hit for the attack (should be the finishing hit for multihits). For throws, the combined damage of all hitboxes that connect is shown instead. Frame values shown are the first frame of the attack.

"Characters Found in Fallback Location" means that the data that is used in HDR is the same as base Ultimate.
